Rabfy allows you to connect your store to **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**. This integration helps you track visitors, view detailed behavior insights, and monitor how people interact with your store.

Follow this full guide to create a GA4 account and connect it to your Rabfy dashboard.

## Step 1: Create a Google Analytics Account

> Skip this step if you already have a Google Analytics account.

1. Go to analytics.google.com and sign in using your Google account.
2. Begin account creation.
3. In the **Account Name** field, enter a name for your Analytics account (e.g., "My Rabfy Store").
4. Under **Account Data Sharing Settings**, select the checkboxes according to your preferences. Recommended:
   - ✅ Technical support
   - ✅ Modeling contributions & business insights
   - ✅ Recommendations for your business
5. Click **Next** to continue.

## Step 2: Create a GA4 Property

1. Enter a **Property Name** (e.g., "Rabfy Store Analytics or Your Store Name Analytics").
2. Choose your **Time Zone** and **Currency** based on your business region (e.g., US Dollar for stores targeting the USA).
3. Click **Next**.

## Step 3: Add Business Details

1. Select your **Industry Category** (e.g., Shopping).
2. Choose your **Business Size**.
3. Click **Next**.

## Step 4: Choose Your Business Objectives

> You can select more than one.

Recommended:

- ✅ Generate leads
- ✅ Drive sales
- ✅ Understand web and/or app traffic
- ✅ View user engagement & retention

Click **Create** to proceed.

## Step 5: Set Up Data Collection

1. Select **Web** as your platform.
2. Enter your Rabfy store URL (e.g., `https://www.yourstore.com`).
3. Give your **Stream Name** (e.g., "Your Store Web").
4. Click **Create Stream**.
5. After the stream is created, copy your **Measurement ID**. It looks like: `G-XXXXXXXXXX`.

This Measurement ID will be used to connect your GA4 account with Rabfy.

## Step 6: Connect GA4 with Rabfy

1. Log in to your [Rabfy Dashboard](https://dashboard.rabfy.com).
2. Go to **Settings** \> **Integrations**.
3. Find and go to **Google Analytics** section.
4. Paste your **Measurement ID** into the input field.
5. Enable the toggle for **Google Analytics**.
6. Click **Save Settings**.

Done\! Your Rabfy store is now connected with Google Analytics 4.

## Step 7: View Your Analytics Data in GA4

1. Go again to [https://analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com/)
2. Select the property you created
3. Click on **Realtime** to see live visitors
4. Use other parameters to see different analytical data.

- Rabfy also provides a built-in Analytics section in the dashboard
