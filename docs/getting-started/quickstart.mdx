This guide helps you start your print-on-demand journey with Rabfy. Follow these easy steps. Each one includes a video to guide you visually. After you complete this guide, you will have a live store ready to market and scale.

## Step by Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Create Your Rabfy Account & Store">
    Start by opening your seller account.

    - Visit <u>https://dashboard.rabfy.com/sign-up</u>
    - Fill in your name, email, password
    - Click continue and verify your email
    - Then give your store name and country (from where your store will be operated)
    - Click create store
    - Log in to your Rabfy dashboard

    <Info>
      You can also continue with your Google account for faster account creation.
